About Prop 30

Everyone who is interested in education in California -- students, alumni, teachers, parents, employers -- should be aware of Proposition 30 on the November 2012 California ballot. It proposes temporary tax increases of 0.25% in the state sales tax and an incremental increase in personal income tax on households earning more than $250,000. If the measure does not pass, a multi-billion dollar cut in state funding to education at all levels will be triggered automatically.
